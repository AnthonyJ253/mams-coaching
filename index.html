MY STORY.

I’m a passionate woman with family, career and fitness aspirations. I believe in achieving happier lives through making meaningful choices, work-life balance and healthy lifestyles. My passion is to help people grow to their true potential.

I’m a wife and mother of 3 amazing sons, certified public accountant, an executive at a Fortune 300 company, life coach, yoga instructor and powerlifter. I juggle a lot but my family, career and fitness makes me happy, provides fulfillment and in many ways provides the life balance I need. My family helps me balance my dedication to career. Fitness helps me get clarity in mind to balance my career and family. My career allows me to support both my family and fitness aspirations. I wasn’t always able to find balance in my aspirations and at certain points in my life I didn’t even know what my aspirations were as I couldn’t navigate through all my thoughts and desires. I’ve been lost many times in my life, as a young wife and mother I got lost in building my purpose around my family, in my career I’ve gotten lost trying to cater toward others expectations and not going for the job opportunities I really wanted because I felt I wasn’t ready or was too afraid to ask for what I deserved, and there were times I lost my connection with fitness because of outside pressures and time constraints.

I’m fortunate to be able to have learned from these challenging times in my life and that I made meaningful choices, which has allowed me to feel amazing in my family relationships, career, body and mind. I want to share my experiences and knowledge with other women and empower them to do the same! As a coach I want to be an unbiased brainstorming partner. I want to help women navigate their thoughts and desires to make purposeful decisions and actions that align with their aspirations in life. I want each woman I coach to see their true potential while being their authentic selves, and then unlock it for the world to see and benefit from.
